Theism: God is the Creator, separate from His creation. Like a painter and a painting. The painter exists, and the painting exists, but they are distinct.
Pantheism: God is the Universe. The universe and God are identical. There is no "extra" God beyond the sum total of all stars, bugs, and galaxies. (God = Universe).
