# Luma

*A quiet exploration of perception, understanding, compassion, wonder, and the extraordinary opportunity of being alive.*

[Live Website](https://pivot.quest/luma/) • [License: CC BY-NC 4.0](LICENSE)

---

## What is Luma?

Luma is an open philosophical project exploring how human beings perceive reality, misunderstand one another, discover clarity, and choose to participate in the ongoing creation, preservation, understanding, and celebration of the world.

Rather than arguing readers toward conclusions, Luma seeks to accompany them into observation.

Its hope is simple:

> We seek to illuminate and share the Light.

## Why is everything here?

This repository intentionally includes not only the finished website, but much of the design work that led to it.

That includes style guides, architectural documents, outlines, planning notes, and other materials created during development.

We believe the construction of thoughtful work can itself be valuable.

Many creative projects reveal only the finished painting.

Luma also shares some of the sketches.

Not because they are perfect.

Not because they are authoritative.

But because philosophy, writing, and design are living crafts, and future builders may find something useful in seeing how one work gradually took shape.

Nothing here should be mistaken for a single "correct" interpretation of the finished work.

The website remains the primary expression of the project.

These materials simply document the path that led there.

---

## AI Collaboration

Luma was created through an iterative collaboration between a human author and multiple AI systems.

Ideas were proposed, challenged, revised, discarded, combined, and refined.

AI did not replace authorship.

It became part of the conversation.

The resulting work reflects continual human judgment about structure, tone, philosophy, coherence, and purpose.

We hope these materials demonstrate one thoughtful example of what collaborative creativity can look like.
